    Project



    Ever since my junior high school, I have been interested in acting and drama, in fact, I have won several storytelling competitions, and have performed and directed a drama in a stage during my junior high school days. Even though I have such passion until now, I never get the chance to perform any drama in my own faculty because of the unsupported environment.

    It all has changed until one of my friends from the Art and Design Faculty offered me for being the main character as well as the narrator for their teaser event called Purwa Carita. Without hesitation, I immediately took the offer. In the teaser, I became a writer of Indonesian folklore who wanted to spread the awareness of our traditional culture to those whose interests had slowly reverted to the Western.



    Directed by two students from the Art and Design Faculty, they truly knew their ways with the cameras. Following their instructions, we could finish the shooting process in just one day. All of us were exhausted, but it was truly worth the effort. In the next day, both of them could finish the teaser skillfully and make the teaser with a dream-like nuance. And the teaser can be accessed here.

    This experience has taught me that we have our very own room to grow, and it is just the matter of the time when we will find the right place with the right people.
